Lemon Pound Cake


Ingredients:

  • 50g of mlted butter
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp of lemon shell
  • 4 tbsp of lemon juice
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 cups of self-rising flour

For the icing:

  • 1 3/4 cups of powdered sugar
  • 5 tbsp of lemon juice

Process:

First Step

Grab a bowl and mix in 50g of butter and 1 cup of sugar.

Second Step

Add the eggs, 4 tbsp of lemon juice, 1 tsp of lemon shell and 1 cup of milk. Whisk it very well.

Third Step

Add 2 cup of self-rising flour and mix it.

Fourth Step

Grab a pan with parchment paper and put in it the previous dough.

Fifth Step

Bake it in a preheated oven at 180Â° for 40 minutes. Let it chill.

Sixth Step

Now we make the icing. Grab a bowl and whisk in it 1 cup of powdered sugar and 5 tbsp of lemon juice.

Seventh Step

Pour the icing over the pound cake.

Eighth Step

And finished! There you have a delicious lemon pound cake in 7 simple steps.
