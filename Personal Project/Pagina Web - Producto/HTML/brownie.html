Brownies


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of butter
  • 175g of chocolate
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 3/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp of vanilla
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup of cocoa powder

Process:

First Step

Microwave 175g of chocolate and 1 cup of butter

Second Step

Add 1 cup of sugar, 3/4 of brown sugar and 1 tbsp of vanilla. Whisk it very well.

Third Step

Add the eggs until they are fully combained.

Fourth Step

Add 1 1/2 cup of flour and 1/3 cup of cocoa powder. Mix it very well.

Fifth Step

Grase a pan and spread the batter.

Sixth Step

Bake it for 30 minutes in a preheated oven at 180Â°C.Let them chill and cut them into pieces. And it is ready!
