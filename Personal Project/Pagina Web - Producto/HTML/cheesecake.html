Oreo Cheesecake


Ingredients:

  • 100g of butter
  • 300g of oreos
  • 300g of cream cheese
  • 150g of cream
  • 6 tbsp of sugar

Process:

First Step

Grab 200g of oreos and crush them into small pieces.

Second Step

Microweave 100g of butter and combine it with the crushed oreos.

Third Step

Grab a pan with film paper and spread the previous dough to create a base. Let it chill in the fridge.

Fourth Step

Put in a bowl 300g of cream cheese, 150g of cream and 6 tbsp of sugar.

Fifth Step

Mix it very well.

Sixth Step

With the oreos left, crush them and combine them into the mix.

Seventh Step

Spread the mix over the base done before and let it chill on the fridge for at list 8 hours.

Eighth Step

Unmold the cheesecake, decorate with more oreos and enjoy!
