Berry Ice Cream


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of berries
  • 1/2 cup of greek yogurt
  • sugar to taste

Process:

First Step

Grab a blender and place 1 cup of berries.

Second Step

Add 1/2 cup of greek yogurt and sugar to taste.

Third Step

Blend it out for 1 minute.

Fourth Step

Place it in a container and freeze it for at list 8 hours.

Fifth Step

Serve it and ready!
