Apple Pie


Ingredients:

  • 150g of butter
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 4 apples

Process:

First Step

Grab a bowl and mix in 150g of butter and 1/2 cup of sugar.

Second Step

Add the egg and the vanilla. Combine it very well.

Third Step

Add 1 1/2 cup of self-rising flour and mix it.

Fourth Step

Grab the apples. Peal them and cut them into thin slices. Place them in a pan.

Fifth Step

Grab the previous dough and place it over the apples.

Sixth Step

Bake it for 30 minutes on a preheated oven at 180Â°. Ready!
