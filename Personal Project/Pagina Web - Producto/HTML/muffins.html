Vanilla Muffins


Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of self-rising flour
  • 2/3 cup of milk
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract

Process:

First Step

Beat up 1/2 cup of butter and 1 cup of sugar

Second Step

Add the egg and whisk it very well.

Third Step

Add 2/3 cup of milk and 1 tsp of vanilla. whisk it until it is nicely combined.

Fourth Step

Add 1 cup of all-purpose flour and 1 cup of self-rising flour. Whisk it perfectly.

Fifth Step

Grab a muffin tin. Place over it the batter until 3/4 of the tin.

Sixth Step

Bake it in a preheated oven at 180Â° for 25 minutes. Finished! Now you have some awesome vanilla muffins!
