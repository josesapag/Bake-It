Waffles


Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp of melted butter
  • 1 cup of self-rising flour
  • 1 tsp of baking powder
  • 2 tbsp of sugar
  • extra butter for the waffle maker

Process:

First Step

Mix 3/4 cup of milk, an egg and 1 tbsp of melted butter.

Second Step

Add 1 cup of self-rising flour, 1 tsp of baking powder and 2 tbsp of sugar. Mix it all very well.

Third Step

Preheat a waffle maker and add butter on it so the waffles wonÂ´t stick. Add 1/2 cup of the batter and cook it for 2 minutes approximately.

Fourth Step

Let them chill. Add toppings and sauces, and it is ready!
