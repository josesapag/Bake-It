Chocolate Chip Cookies


Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup of melted butter
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp of vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 of all-purpos flour
  • 1/2 cup of chocolate chips

Process:

First Step

Beat up 1/2 cup of mlted butter, 1/2 cup of brown sugar and 1/4 cup of sugar.

Second Step

Add the egg and 1 tbsp of vanilla. Whisk it very well.

Third Step

Add 1 1/2 of all-purpose flour and mix it without over-beating the batter.

Fourth Step

Grab a pan with parchment paper. Place the batter with a spoon on the pan.

Fifth Step

Bake it in a prehated oven at 180Â° for 15 minutes. Let them chill, and ready!
