Vitamin C Smoothie


Ingredients:

  • 1 peach
  • 1/2 cup of orange juice
  • 1/2 a grated carrot
  • sugar to taste

Process:

First Step

Grab a blender and place 1 cutted and pealed peach.

Second Step

Add 1/2 cup of orange juice, 1/2 a grated carrot and sugar to taste.

Third Step

Place some ice cubes.

Fourth Step

Blend it for 1 minute aproximately.

Fifth Step

Serve it and ready!
