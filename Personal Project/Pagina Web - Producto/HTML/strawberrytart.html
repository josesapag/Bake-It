Strawberry Tart


Ingredients:

  • 100g of butter
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup of dulce de leche
  • 1/2 cup of cream
  • strawberries

Process:

First Step

Mix in 100g of butter and 1/2 cup of sugar in a bowl.

Second Step

Add the egg and the vanilla. Combine it very well.

Third Step

Add 1 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour and mix it.

Fourth Step

Grab some mini rounded pans and cover them with some partchment paper. Fill them with the previous dough.

Fifth Step

Bake it at 180Â° for 25 minutes. Let it chill.

Sixth Step

Once baked, take the base from the pan. Spread some dulce de leche over it.

Seventh Step

Now beat up the cream until it is quite firm.

Eighth Step

Spread the beated cream over the dulce de leche.

Ninth Step

Cut some strawberries into slices. Place them over the tart.

Tenth Step

And ready! Let it chill for some minutes and it is ready to eat.
